Fish's Expedition

Fish's expedition With Asia Waves

From the Fisherman

Prior to embarking on the fishing expedition, our representatives collect up-to-date weather data to assess the ideal duration, appropriate timings, and necessary equipment. They guarantee the natural capture and preservation of the fish until their arrival at our delivery centers.

Preparation and Freezing

Once the fisherman arrives at our delivery centers, our team of ice experts begins preserving fish quality through the use of ice and essential freezing techniques. We gather daily production from all our centers, adhering to global standards in preparation and freezing processes.

Delivering to Our Clients

Following the freezing and packaging process, the ultimate step includes transporting our fish to clients using diverse means of transportation. Asia Waves is dedicated to offering comprehensive services to its clients, striving to be their nearest and finest source while ensuring product quality.
